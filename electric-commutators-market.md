Groove commutators, on the other hand, have grooves to guide the brushes, enhancing performance in larger applications. Other market types may include specialized designs catering to unique motor requirements. These variations address diverse operational needs, influencing efficiency, durability, and application suitability in electric motor systems.</p></p>
