<p><p>The Single-Sided ID Card Printer market features two primary types: Inkjet Printers and Dye Sub Printers. Inkjet printers utilize liquid ink to produce vibrant, detailed images, making them ideal for low-volume applications or personal use. In contrast, Dye Sub printers use a heat transfer process to deliver high-quality, durable prints, suitable for high-volume production environments. Both types cater to various industries, including corporate, educational, and government sectors, focusing on efficient, high-quality ID card production tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
