<p><p>Hydraulic flange spreaders are essential tools used in maintenance and assembly operations involving flanged connections. The market is segmented into two main types: split type and integral type. Split type spreaders feature two separate jaws that can operate independently, allowing for precise flange separation in tight spaces. In contrast, integral type spreaders consist of a single unit, offering a streamlined design for quicker setup and operation. Both types enhance safety and efficiency in flange management, catering to different industrial needs.</p></p>
