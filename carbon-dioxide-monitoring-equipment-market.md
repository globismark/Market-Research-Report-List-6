<p><strong>The Carbon Dioxide Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mainstream</li><li>Sidestream</li><li>Microstream</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon dioxide monitoring equipment market is categorized into three primary types: mainstream, sidestream, and microstream. Mainstream monitors directly measure CO2 from airway gases, providing real-time analysis during ventilation. Sidestream monitors sample gas from a respiratory circuit, analyzing it separately, which allows for flexible placement but may introduce delays. Microstream technology is a compact, portable option that utilizes low sample flow rates for accurate measurements, making it ideal for point-of-care settings and enhancing patient safety in various clinical environments.</p></p>
