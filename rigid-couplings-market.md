<p><p>Rigid couplings are devices used to connect two shafts without permitting any relative motion. The sleeve coupling features a cylindrical body that directly connects the shafts, providing stability and alignment. Flanged couplings consist of two flanges that are bolted together, allowing for easy disassembly and maintenance. Clamped couplings utilize a clamping mechanism to secure the shafts, accommodating slight misalignments. Each type is essential for various industrial applications, ensuring reliable power transmission and minimizing vibration.</p></p>
