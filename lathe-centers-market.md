<p><strong>The Lathe Centers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Fixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lathe centers play a crucial role in machining operations by holding and stabilizing workpieces. The market can be divided into two main types: rotary type and fixed type. Rotary lathe centers allow for continuous rotation of the workpiece, enhancing efficiency for tasks requiring 360-degree access. In contrast, fixed lathe centers provide a stable, non-rotating base, ensuring precision for operations that require secure positioning. Both types serve distinct applications in the machining industry, catering to varied manufacturing needs and processes.</p></p>
