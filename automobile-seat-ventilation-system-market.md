<p><strong>The Automobile Seat Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blow</li><li>Suction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile seat ventilation system market comprises two primary types: blow and suction systems. Blow systems work by pushing air through the seat cushions, enhancing comfort by cooling the occupant directly. In contrast, suction systems draw air away from the seat, creating a cooling effect by reducing humidity and temperature. Both methods aim to improve passenger comfort during warm weather, but they differ in airflow mechanics and overall efficiency, catering to varying consumer preferences and vehicle designs.</p></p>
