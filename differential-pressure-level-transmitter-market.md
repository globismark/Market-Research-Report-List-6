<p><p>The differential pressure level transmitter market is categorized into three main types: straight rod type transmitters, flange type transmitters, and screw-type transmitters. Straight rod transmitters offer simplicity and durability for various industrial applications. Flange-type transmitters provide a robust connection to tanks or vessels, ensuring reliable readings under high-pressure conditions. Screw-type transmitters are designed for easy installation and maintenance, making them suitable for limited space environments. Each type serves unique application requirements in process industries for accurate level measurement.</p></p>
