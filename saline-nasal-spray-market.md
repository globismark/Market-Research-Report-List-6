<p><strong>The Saline Nasal Spray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic Sea Water Nasal Spray</li><li>Hypertonic Sea Water Nasal Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The saline nasal spray market comprises two main types: isotonic and hypertonic sea water nasal sprays. Isotonic sea water nasal sprays contain a salt concentration similar to that of human cells, providing gentle hydration and relief from nasal congestion without irritation. In contrast, hypertonic sea water nasal sprays have a higher salt concentration, drawing excess fluid from the nasal tissues, thus offering more intense decongestion. Both types are effective in treating nasal issues, allergies, and improving overall sinus health.</p></p>
