<p><p>The copper pipes market consists of three primary types: Type K, Type L, and Type M. Type K pipes have the thickest walls, ideal for underground and heavy-duty applications, offering maximum durability. Type L pipes have medium thickness, commonly used for residential plumbing and water distribution. Type M pipes feature the thinnest walls and are primarily used for low-pressure applications, such as drain, waste, and vent systems. Each type serves distinct purposes based on durability, pressure requirements, and installation settings.</p></p>
