Dispersive NIR Spectroscopy involves separating light into its component wavelengths, providing accurate measurements for solid and liquid samples. Other technologies include Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTF) and Filter-based systems, which offer specific advantages in terms of speed and application versatility. Together, these types cater to diverse analytical needs in scientific and industrial sectors.</p></p>
