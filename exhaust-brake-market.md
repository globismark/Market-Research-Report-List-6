<p><p>The Exhaust Brake market includes two main types: **Exhaust Pipe Exhaust Brake** and **Engine Exhaust Brake**. Exhaust Pipe Exhaust Brakes are installed in the exhaust system, restricting exhaust flow to create backpressure, slowing the vehicle. Engine Exhaust Brakes are integrated into the engine, using valve mechanisms to control exhaust flow for braking. Both types enhance vehicle safety, reduce wear on traditional brakes, and are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and buses for efficient downhill speed control.</p></p>
