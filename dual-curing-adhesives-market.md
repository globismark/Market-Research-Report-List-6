<p><p>The dual-curing adhesives market primarily consists of two types: UV curing and visible light-curing adhesives. UV curing adhesives utilize ultraviolet light to initiate a rapid polymerization process, providing strong bonds in a short time. Conversely, visible light-curing adhesives require light in the visible spectrum, making them more compatible with various substrates and environments. Both types offer excellent adhesion, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, catering to diverse applications in industries like automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
