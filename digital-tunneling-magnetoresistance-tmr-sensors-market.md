<p><p>Digital Tunneling Magnetoresistance (TMR) sensors are categorized into three types based on their magnetic field response: omnipolar, bipolar, and unipolar. Omnipolar sensors can detect both north and south poles of a magnetic field, offering versatile applications. Bipolar sensors respond to both polarities but typically toggle between two output states. Unipolar sensors, in contrast, react only to one magnetic pole, providing a single output state. Each type fulfills specific requirements in various industries, enhancing functionality in sensor technologies.</p></p>
