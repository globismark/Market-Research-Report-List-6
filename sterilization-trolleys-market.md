<p><p>Sterilization trolleys are essential in healthcare settings for transporting sterilized instruments. The market is divided into two main types: closed-structure and open-structure trolleys. Closed-structure trolleys feature fully enclosed compartments that protect instruments from contamination, making them ideal for maintaining sterility during transport. In contrast, open-structure trolleys have exposed surfaces that facilitate quick access to items but may increase contamination risk. Both types serve specific operational needs, balancing accessibility and infection control in clinical environments.</p></p>
