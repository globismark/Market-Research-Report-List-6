<p><p>Activated Carbon Fiber (ACF) is a specialized form of activated carbon with high surface area and adsorption properties, used for air, water purification, and various industrial applications. The ACF market is categorized into four types: </p><p>1. **Pitch Based ACF** - Derived from petroleum or coal tar pitch, offering high mechanical strength and thermal stability.  </p><p>2. **Polyacrylonitrile (PAN) Based ACF** - Known for superior adsorption capacity and flexibility.  </p><p>3. **Viscose Staple Based ACF** - Made from regenerated cellulose, balancing cost and performance.  </p><p>4. **Other ACF** - Includes samples from alternative raw materials or special treatments, expanding application potential.</p></p>
