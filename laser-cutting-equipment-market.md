<p><p>The laser cutting equipment market consists of two primary types: CNC cutting equipment and manual cutting equipment. CNC (Computer Numerical Control) cutting equipment is automated and uses computer programming for precise and efficient cutting of materials, making it ideal for large-scale production and complex designs. In contrast, manual cutting equipment requires direct operator involvement, offering flexibility for small-scale or one-off projects but usually lacks the speed and precision of CNC machines. Both types serve distinct needs within various industries.</p></p>
