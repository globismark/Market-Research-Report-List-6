<p><p>The nucleic acid multiplex assays market is categorized into three main types: planar nucleic acid assays, bead-based nucleic acid assays, and other nucleic acid assays. Planar assays involve immobilizing nucleic acids on a flat surface, enabling the simultaneous detection of multiple targets. Bead-based assays utilize microspheres to enhance sensitivity and allow for higher throughput. Other assays encompass various alternative platforms and methodologies, including microfluidics and chip-based technologies, which offer unique benefits in multiplexing capabilities and analytical performance.</p></p>
