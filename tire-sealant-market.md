<p><p>The tire sealant market includes latex-based and latex-free sealants. Latex-based sealants use natural rubber latex, offering strong adhesion and flexibility, ideal for sealing punctures quickly. They are eco-friendly but may cause allergic reactions. Latex-free sealants, made from synthetic polymers, are hypoallergenic and suitable for users with latex allergies. They provide durable sealing and are often preferred for long-term use. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing performance, safety, and environmental considerations in the tire repair industry.</p></p>
