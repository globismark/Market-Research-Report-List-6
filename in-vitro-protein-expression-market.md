<p><p>The in vitro protein expression market encompasses various systems, each suited for specific applications. Yeast expression leverages simplicity and eukaryotic post-translational modifications. Mammalian expression offers complex protein folding, ideal for therapeutic proteins. Algae expression is environmentally sustainable for producing bioactive compounds. Insect expression uses baculovirus systems for high yields. Bacterial expression is efficient for simple proteins but may lack post-translational modifications. Cell-free expression allows rapid protein synthesis without living cells, enabling quick prototyping and flexibility in research applications.</p></p>
