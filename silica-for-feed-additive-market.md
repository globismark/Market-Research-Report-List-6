<p><p>The silica for feed additive market comprises three main types: powder, micro-pearl, and super-fine powder. Powder silica serves as a traditional additive, enhancing the flow and stability of feed. Micro-pearl silica features a smaller particle size, improving digestibility and nutrient absorption in animals. Super-fine powder silica offers even finer particles, providing superior surface area for effective moisture control and anti-caking properties, thereby optimizing feed quality and performance in livestock and aquaculture applications.</p></p>
