<p><p>The pharmaceutical tablet press machine market is divided into two primary types: single-station and multiple-station machines. Single-station tablet presses are designed for low-volume production, ideal for research and development or small batch operations, as they allow for quick adjustments and easy maintenance. In contrast, multiple-station tablet presses, often known as rotary presses, are suited for high-volume manufacturing, featuring multiple compression stations that enhance efficiency and productivity, making them essential for large-scale pharmaceutical production.</p></p>
