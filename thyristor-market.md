<p><p>The thyristor market is categorized into unidirectional and bidirectional thyristors. Unidirectional thyristors, primarily used in DC applications, allow current to flow in one direction, making them ideal for purposes like battery chargers and motor drives. Bidirectional thyristors, on the other hand, can conduct current in both directions, suited for AC applications such as lighting controls and power regulators. Both types find applications across various industries, driving demand due to their efficiency and reliability in controlling electrical power.</p></p>
