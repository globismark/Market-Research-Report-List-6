<p><p>The microfluidic chip customization service market can be categorized primarily into pressure-driven and electric field-driven types. Pressure-driven systems utilize fluid pressure to move liquids through microchannels, offering precise control over flow rates and volume. In contrast, electric field-driven systems employ electrokinetic forces to manipulate fluids, facilitating rapid analysis and mixing at the microscale. Both types enable tailored chip designs for various applications, including biomedical diagnostics and chemical analysis, enhancing functionality and efficiency in microfluidic devices.</p></p>
