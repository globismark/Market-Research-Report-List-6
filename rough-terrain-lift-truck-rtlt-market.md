<p><p>Rough Terrain Lift Trucks (RTLT) are specialized forklifts designed for outdoor use on uneven surfaces. The two primary types are Telescopic RTLTs and Masted RTLTs. Telescopic RTLTs feature extendable booms allowing for greater reach and versatility, ideal for lifting loads over obstacles. Masted RTLTs have vertical masts for stability and lifting capabilities, suitable for heavy loads on rugged terrains. Both types enhance efficiency and safety in construction, agriculture, and landscaping applications, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
