<p><p>The robotic lawnmowers market can be segmented into two main types based on their cutting systems: pivoting blades and fixed blades. Pivoting blade systems utilize rotating blades that can tilt, allowing for adaptive cutting angles and improved maneuverability over uneven terrain. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and lawn care needs, influencing their market presence and growth potential.</p></p>
