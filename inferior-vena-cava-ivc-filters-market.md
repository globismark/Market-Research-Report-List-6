<p><ul><li>Permanent IVC Filters</li><li>Retrievable IVC Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inferior Vena Cava (IVC) filter market comprises two primary types: permanent IVC filters and retrievable IVC filters. Permanent IVC filters are designed for long-term implantation to prevent pulmonary embolism in patients with ongoing risks. In contrast, retrievable IVC filters can be temporarily placed and later removed, offering flexibility for patients whose risk of embolism may be transient. Both types are utilized based on individual patient needs, underlying conditions, and duration of risk.</p></p>
