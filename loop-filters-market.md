<p><p>Loop filters are essential components in control systems, particularly in phase-locked loops (PLLs). The market for loop filters can be divided into passive and active types. Passive loop filters utilize resistors, capacitors, and inductors without active components, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, active loop filters incorporate operational amplifiers, providing enhanced performance and flexibility but at a higher complexity and cost. Both types serve critical roles in various applications, including communications, electronics, and signal processing, catering to different performance and design requirements.</p></p>
