<p><p>Interventional oncology encompasses various minimally invasive procedures for treating cancer. Radiation therapy utilizes targeted radiation to destroy tumor cells, ensuring minimal damage to surrounding tissue. Ablation involves techniques like radiofrequency or microwave energy to heat and destroy tumors. Particle embolization involves delivering small particles to block blood flow to tumors, inducing necrosis. Together, these modalities offer effective alternatives to traditional surgery, improving patient outcomes and reducing recovery times in oncology treatments.</p></p>
