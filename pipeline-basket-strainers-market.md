<p><p>The Pipeline Basket Strainers market comprises two main types: Simplex Basket Strainers and Duplex Basket Strainers. Simplex Basket Strainers are designed for single-line applications, making them suitable for various fluids in pipelines where space is limited. Conversely, Duplex Basket Strainers feature two parallel strainers, allowing for continuous operation during cleaning or maintenance, ideal for critical applications. Both types are essential for protecting equipment from debris, ensuring system efficiency, and minimizing downtime in industrial processes.</p></p>
