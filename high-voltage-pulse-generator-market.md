<p><p>The high voltage pulse generator market is categorized into three main types. Anode high voltage pulse generators produce positive voltage pulses, commonly used in applications like accelerators and gas discharge tubes. Cathode high voltage pulse generators generate negative voltage pulses, often employed in electron beam applications and plasma research. Bipolar high voltage pulse generators can produce both positive and negative pulses, making them versatile for various uses, including material testing and semiconductor applications, due to their ability to control pulse polarity.</p></p>
