<p><ul><li>Dissolvable Wireless Sensor</li><li>Wearable and Implantable Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brain monitoring sensors market comprises three main types: dissolvable wireless sensors, wearable sensors, and implantable sensors. Dissolvable wireless sensors offer a temporary solution that disintegrates after use, minimizing invasiveness. Wearable sensors, such as headbands or caps, monitor brain activity externally and provide real-time data for various applications, from fitness to medical diagnostics. Implantable sensors are surgically placed within the brain, allowing for long-term monitoring and deeper insights into neurological conditions. Each type serves distinct needs in brain health management.</p></p>
