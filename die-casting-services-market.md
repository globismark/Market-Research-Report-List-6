<p><strong>The Die Casting Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Gravity</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die casting services encompass three primary market types: high pressure, gravity, and low pressure. High pressure die casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold under high pressure, resulting in precise and detailed components, commonly used in automotive and electronics. Gravity die casting utilizes gravity to fill the mold, suitable for larger, less complex parts. Low pressure die casting employs lower pressure to control metal flow, minimizing defects and enabling thicker sections, making it ideal for structural applications. Each method offers distinct advantages based on project requirements.</p></p>
