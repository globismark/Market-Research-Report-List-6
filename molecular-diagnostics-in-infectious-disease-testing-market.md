<p><p>Molecular diagnostics in infectious disease testing encompasses various advanced technologies. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies specific DNA sequences for precise pathogen detection. Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Technology (INAAT) allows for rapid amplification without temperature cycling. Microarrays enable simultaneous detection of multiple pathogens through hybridization techniques. Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) provides comprehensive genomic insights by sequencing entire genomes or targeted regions. Hybridization methods detect specific nucleic acid sequences, while other emerging technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities, expanding options for timely infectious disease management.</p></p>
