<p><strong>The C-SiC Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carbon Particles and Silicon Carbide (Cp/SiC) Composites</li><li>Carbon Fiber and Silicon Carbide (Cf/SiC) Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>C-SiC composites are advanced materials that combine carbon with silicon carbide to enhance performance in high-temperature and high-strength applications. Carbon Particles and Silicon Carbide (Cp/SiC) composites utilize dispersed carbon particles within a silicon carbide matrix, offering improved thermal conductivity and strength. In contrast, Carbon Fiber and Silicon Carbide (Cf/SiC) composites integrate carbon fibers for exceptional tensile strength and lightweight properties, making them ideal for aerospace, automotive, and industrial applications where reliability and efficiency are crucial.</p></p>
