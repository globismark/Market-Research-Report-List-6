<p><strong>The Electric Three Wheelers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Wheel Motors</li><li>Center Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric three-wheelers are gaining popularity for urban mobility, with two main motor configurations: **Side Wheel Motors** and **Center Motors**. Side Wheel Motors integrate motors directly into the rear wheels, offering better weight distribution, simpler design, and lower maintenance. Center Motors place the motor centrally, connected to the wheels via a drivetrain, providing higher torque and efficiency for heavier loads. Both types cater to different needs—Side Wheel Motors suit lightweight, short-distance travel, while Center Motors excel in commercial or high-load applications, balancing performance and durability.</p></p>
