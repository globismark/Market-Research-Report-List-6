<p><p>The airport radar market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions. Surveillance radars provide broad coverage for air traffic management and security. Weather radars detect atmospheric conditions to aid in flight safety. Approach radars assist in guiding aircraft during landing. Secondary radars, using transponders, enhance identification and tracking. Primary radars evaluate the presence of objects without relying on transponder signals. Together, these radar types ensure efficient airport operations, enhance safety, and support the growing demands of air travel.</p></p>
