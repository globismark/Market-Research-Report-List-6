<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>Premise Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hotel Reservation Software market is primarily categorized into two types: Cloud-Based and Premise-Based solutions. Cloud-Based software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access reservations and data from any device with internet connectivity, promoting flexibility and lower upfront costs. In contrast, Premise-Based software is installed locally on the hotel’s hardware, offering enhanced control and security but requiring higher initial investment and maintenance. Both types cater to varying business needs and operational preferences within the hospitality industry.</p></p>
