<p><p>The Premium Intraocular Lens (IOL) market consists of three primary types. Multifocal IOLs provide multiple vision zones, enabling clear sight at various distances. Accommodating IOLs mimic the natural eye's ability to focus, adjusting to provide clarity at different ranges. Extended Depth of Focus IOLs enhance visual acuity by extending the range of clear vision, reducing the need for corrective eyewear. These advanced lenses cater to patients seeking improved vision quality following cataract surgery or refractive procedures.</p></p>
