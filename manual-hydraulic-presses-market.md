<p><p>The manual hydraulic presses market comprises four key types: </p><p>1. **Closed Punch**: These presses provide complete containment for the workpiece, ensuring precision during the operation.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Semi-Open Punch**: Offering a balance between accessibility and containment, this type allows limited exposure for easier part loading.</p><p>3. **Tiltable Punch**: These presses are designed to tilt, enhancing worker ergonomics and facilitating easier material handling.</p><p>4. **Open Punch**: Featuring an unobstructed design, open punch presses allow maximum access, ideal for larger, bulkier items. Each type caters to specific operational needs in manufacturing and fabrication.</p></p>
