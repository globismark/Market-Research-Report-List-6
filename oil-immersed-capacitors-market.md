<p><p>The oil-immersed capacitors market features two primary types: round and upright capacitors. Round oil-immersed capacitors are typically cylindrical in shape and designed for specific applications requiring compactness and efficient heat dissipation. In contrast, upright oil-immersed capacitors have a vertical configuration, providing a larger surface area for cooling and accommodating higher voltage levels. Both types are used in power systems, transformers, and various electrical equipment, offering reliable performance in diverse industrial and utility applications requiring insulation and energy storage.</p></p>
