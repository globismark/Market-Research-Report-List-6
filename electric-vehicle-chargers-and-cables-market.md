<p><p>The electric vehicle charger and cable market is primarily divided into AC and DC charging types. AC charging utilizes alternating current and is typically slower, suitable for home and public charging stations, allowing vehicles to recharge overnight. In contrast, DC charging uses direct current, providing rapid charging capabilities ideal for commercial and highway stations, significantly reducing downtime. Each type serves distinct user needs, with AC suited for convenience and DC designed for quick turnaround, driving wider adoption of electric vehicles.</p></p>
