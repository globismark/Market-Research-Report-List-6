<p><p>The Free Triiodothyronine Test Kit market consists of various testing methodologies, primarily including Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) and Time-resolved Immunoassay (TRIA). CLIA utilizes light emission to detect hormone levels, offering high sensitivity and rapid results, making it a popular choice in clinical settings. In contrast, TRIA employs time-resolved fluorescent probes, allowing for enhanced specificity and detection of low concentrations. Both methods play a crucial role in diagnosing thyroid disorders, driving demand in healthcare facilities and laboratories.</p></p>
