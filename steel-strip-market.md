<p><p>The steel strip market is primarily divided into two types: hot rolled and cold rolled. Hot rolled steel strips are produced by rolling at high temperatures, which makes them more malleable and suitable for various applications, typically featuring a rougher surface. In contrast, cold rolled steel strips are processed at room temperature, resulting in tighter tolerances, smoother finishes, and enhanced mechanical properties. Each type serves distinct needs in industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing, influencing their demand dynamics.</p></p>
