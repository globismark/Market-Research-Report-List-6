<p><ul><li>Flow Type</li><li>Back Pressure Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air micrometers market is primarily categorized into three types: flow type, back pressure type, and others. Flow type micrometers measure the airflow passing through a small orifice, providing precise displacement readings. Back pressure type micrometers gauge the pressure difference caused by airflow, offering insights into flow characteristics. "Others" encompasses varied designs for specialized applications, such as digital or electronic air micrometers, which cater to unique measurement needs in industries like precision engineering and manufacturing.</p></p>
