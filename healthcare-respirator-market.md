<p><p>The healthcare respirator market comprises two primary types: flat-fold and cup style respirators. Flat-fold respirators are designed to be compact and easily stored, allowing for convenient transport and usage. They are typically made from flexible materials that enable them to conform closely to the wearer’s face. In contrast, cup style respirators maintain a structured shape, providing a more rigid fit around the face. Both types are essential for ensuring effective filtration and protection in healthcare settings.</p></p>
