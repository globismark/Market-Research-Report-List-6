<p><p>Medium Voltage Ring Main Unit (RMU) switchgear types include Solid Insulated, Gas Insulated, and Air Insulated configurations. Solid Insulated RMUs use materials like epoxy to provide robust insulation and compact designs, ideal for space-constrained environments. Gas Insulated RMUs utilize insulating gas, offering high reliability and minimal maintenance, suitable for urban settings. Air Insulated RMUs rely on air for insulation and are commonly used in outdoor applications, known for their cost-effectiveness and ease of access for maintenance. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>
