<p><strong>The Low Voltage AC Drive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The low voltage AC drive market encompasses primarily two inverter types: Voltage Source Inverter (VSI) and Current Source Inverter (CSI). VSI utilizes a fixed DC voltage to produce AC output, making it suitable for various applications, including fan and pump control. In contrast, CSI relies on a fixed DC current to generate output, leading to higher fault tolerance and simpler designs, primarily used in applications requiring precise torque control, like in heavy machinery. Each inverter caters to distinct operational requirements and industry demands.</p></p>
