<p><p>The SiC fibers market is categorized into three primary types based on the matrix materials used. Polymer Matrix Composites (PMCs) utilize organic polymers to offer lightweight and flexibility, ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMCs) use ceramic materials to provide high-temperature resistance and durability, suitable for aerospace and energy sectors. Metal Matrix Composites (MMCs) incorporate metals, enhancing strength and thermal conductivity, making them effective for industrial applications. Each type serves distinct industries and application requirements.</p></p>
