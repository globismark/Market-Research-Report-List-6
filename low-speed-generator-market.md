<p><ul><li>Synchronous Generator</li><li>Asynchronous Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-speed generator market comprises primarily two types: synchronous and asynchronous generators. Synchronous generators operate at a constant speed, generating electricity in sync with the grid frequency, making them ideal for applications requiring stable output. In contrast, asynchronous generators, or induction generators, operate at variable speeds and are commonly used in wind power systems. Both types serve crucial roles in renewable energy sectors, providing efficient energy conversion while adapting to different operational conditions and energy demands.</p></p>
