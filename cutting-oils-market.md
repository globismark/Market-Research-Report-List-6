<p><p>The **Cutting Oils Market** comprises two primary types: **Water Soluble Cutting Oils** and **Neat Cutting Oils**. **Water Soluble Cutting Oils** are emulsions diluted with water, offering excellent cooling properties, ideal for high-speed machining and reducing heat generation. They are cost-effective and environmentally friendly. **Neat Cutting Oils**, on the other hand, are undiluted, providing superior lubrication and protection against wear, suitable for heavy-duty operations. Both types enhance tool life, improve surface finish, and optimize machining efficiency, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
