<p><strong>The Portable Ultrasound Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-mode</li><li>B-mode or 2D mode</li><li>C-mode</li><li>M-mode</li><li>Doppler mode</li><li>Pulse inversion mode</li><li>Harmonic mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable ultrasound machine market encompasses various types, each serving specific diagnostic purposes. A-mode provides a one-dimensional view for measuring distances. B-mode (2D) generates grayscale images for anatomical visualization. C-mode focuses on cross-sectional images, while M-mode captures motion over time. Doppler mode evaluates blood flow and velocity. Pulse inversion enhances image resolution by canceling out artifacts, and Harmonic mode improves image quality by utilizing harmonic frequencies. Collectively, these modes cater to diverse clinical applications and diagnostic needs.</p></p>
