<p><p>The construction safety helmets market comprises three main types: ABS protective helmets, HDPE protective helmets, and FRP protective helmets. ABS helmets are lightweight and impact-resistant, making them suitable for general construction work. HDPE helmets offer excellent durability and resistance to chemicals, ideal for harsh environments. FRP helmets provide high strength, lightweight properties, and better insulation, often used in electrical or high-temperature settings. Each type caters to specific safety needs, ensuring effective protection for workers in various construction scenarios.</p></p>
