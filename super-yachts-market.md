<p><p>The super yacht market is divided into monohull and multihull types. Monohull yachts feature a single hull, offering stability, traditional design, and efficient long-range cruising. They dominate the market due to their classic appeal and performance in rough seas. Multihull yachts, such as catamarans or trimarans, have two or more hulls, providing greater stability, spacious interiors, and reduced draft for shallow waters. They appeal to those seeking modern designs, comfort, and fuel efficiency, gaining popularity in leisure and charter markets. Both cater to luxury lifestyles but differ in design and functionality.</p></p>
