<p><p>The AIM (Acrylic Impact Modifier) market comprises two primary types: Ordinary AIM and Low Temperature Resistance AIM. Ordinary AIM is designed to enhance the toughness and impact resistance of polymers at standard conditions, commonly used in applications like automotive and construction materials. In contrast, Low Temperature Resistance AIM is specifically formulated to maintain its impact performance in colder environments, making it ideal for outdoor applications and products exposed to harsh weather. Together, these modifiers enhance material durability across diverse industries.</p></p>
