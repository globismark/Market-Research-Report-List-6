<p><p>The circuit simulation software market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Analog Circuit Simulation Software** focuses on simulating continuous signals and non-linear components, essential for designing analog systems like amplifiers and oscillators. </p><p>2. **Digital Circuit Simulation Software** emulates discrete signals, aiding in the design and verification of digital circuits such as logic gates and microcontrollers. </p><p>3. **Mixed-Mode Simulation Software** combines both analog and digital simulations, allowing for comprehensive testing of complex circuits that incorporate both types of signals, catering to modern design needs.</p></p>
