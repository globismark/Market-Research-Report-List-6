<p><strong>The Municipal Solid Waste Incineration Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moving Grate</li><li>Rotary-kiln</li><li>Fluidized Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The municipal solid waste incineration market comprises three main technologies: moving grate, rotary-kiln, and fluidized bed. Moving grate systems use a conveyor mechanism to transport waste while facilitating combustion, making them suitable for diverse waste types. Rotary-kiln incinerators operate by rotating a cylindrical chamber, promoting thorough mixing and efficient burning of solid waste. Fluidized bed incinerators utilize a bed of sand particles fluidized by air, allowing for uniform heat distribution and enhanced combustion efficiency, accommodating varying waste compositions effectively.</p></p>
