<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dispersion Polyurethane Adhesive market is primarily categorized into two types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset adhesives undergo a curing process, resulting in a rigid, heat-resistant bond that maintains its strength over time. They are ideal for applications requiring durability and resistance to environmental factors. In contrast, thermoplastic adhesives remain flexible after cooling and can be reprocessed upon heating. This category is suitable for applications needing flexibility and ease of application, often used in industries like packaging and automotive.</p></p>
