<p><strong>The Electromagnetic Shock Absorber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Active Shock Absorber</li><li>Semi-Active Shock Absorber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic shock absorber market is primarily divided into two categories: pure active shock absorbers and semi-active shock absorbers. Pure active shock absorbers use sensors and actuators to continuously adjust damping forces in real-time, providing optimal performance under varying conditions. In contrast, semi-active shock absorbers feature adjustable damping but rely on a passive system to absorb shocks, responding to changing road conditions without actively changing the physical components. Both types enhance vehicle stability and comfort by managing vibrations and impacts effectively.</p></p>
