<p><p>The premium wireless routers market is categorized into three main types: Single Band Wireless Routers, Dual Band Wireless Routers, and Tri Band Wireless Routers. Single Band routers operate on a single frequency, typically 2.4 GHz, suitable for basic internet tasks. Dual Band routers use both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, offering improved performance and reduced interference for multitasking. Tri Band routers add an additional 5 GHz band, ideal for high-demand environments, supporting multiple devices with minimal lag and enhanced speed.</p></p>
