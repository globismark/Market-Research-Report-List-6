<p><ul><li>Polyurethane Emulsion Coating</li><li>Polyurethane Dispersion Coating</li><li>Polyurethane Aqueous Solution Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterborne polyurethane coatings market encompasses three main types: polyurethane emulsion coatings, which consist of fine droplets of polymer in water, providing excellent flexibility and durability; polyurethane dispersion coatings, characterized by the uniform distribution of polymers in water, offering superior adhesion and low VOC emissions; and polyurethane aqueous solution coatings, where polymers are dissolved in water, allowing for easy application while maintaining good chemical resistance. These types cater to diverse applications in automotive, construction, and furniture sectors.</p></p>
