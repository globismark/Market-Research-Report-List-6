<p><p>The stable isotope ratio mass spectrometer (IRMS) market is segmented into several types: GC-IRMS, EA-IRMS, LC-IRMS, and others. GC-IRMS applies gas chromatography to analyze volatile compounds, providing precise isotopic ratios. EA-IRMS is employed for elemental analysis, particularly of solid samples, allowing for detailed organic and inorganic assessments. LC-IRMS utilizes liquid chromatography for the analysis of liquid samples, enhancing specificity. Other types include innovations and hybrid systems that cater to specialized applications in environmental science, geochemistry, and biology.</p></p>
