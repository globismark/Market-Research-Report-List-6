<p><p>Wireless antennas play a crucial role in the automotive market, supporting communication systems such as navigation, infotainment, and vehicle-to-everything (V2X) connectivity. The UHF (Ultra High Frequency) antenna operates in the 300 MHz to 3 GHz range, enabling reliable communication over longer distances, ideal for smart vehicles and real-time data exchange. In contrast, VHF (Very High Frequency) antennas, functioning between 30 MHz and 300 MHz, are typically used for radio broadcasts and vehicle tracking, offering enhanced signal penetration in urban environments.</p></p>
